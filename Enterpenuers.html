Bill Gates

William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American business magnate,
software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist. He is a co-founder of
Microsoft, along with his late childhood friend Paul Allen.[2][3] During his career
at Microsoft, Gates held the positions of chairman, chief executive officer (CEO),
president and chief software architect, while also being the largest individual
shareholder until May 2014.[4] He was a major entrepreneur of the microcomputer
revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.
